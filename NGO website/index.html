About Us

A successful "About Us" page for a non-governmental organization typically includes the following elements: Mission, vision, and values: Clearly state your NGO's purpose and what it hopes to achieve in the long term. This helps visitors understand your motivation and core principles. The founding story: Share the narrative of how and why your NGO was started. A personal, emotional origin story is a powerful way to build an authentic connection with your audience. Impact and achievements: Use compelling stories, testimonials, and tangible statistics to showcase the difference your organization has made. Quantifiable results, such as "11,000 families served," add credibility.
